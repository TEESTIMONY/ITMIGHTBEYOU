Digital Empowerment Packages

Explore our comprehensive digital empowerment packages designed to equip Nigerians with the tools and skills needed to thrive in today's digital economy.

Empowering Digital Futures

Our Digital Empowerment Initiative aims to provide Nigerians with both the tools and knowledge needed to succeed in an increasingly digital world. Each package is carefully designed to address specific needs and skill levels.

Winners of our quarterly draws can choose from any of these packages as an alternative to the cash prize. Additionally, all registered members get access to our basic digital training resources.

How to Qualify

To be eligible for these digital empowerment packages:

  1. Register as a member with a one-time fee
  2. Participate in our quarterly draws with a contribution per draw
  3. If selected as a winner, you can choose between the cash prize or any of the digital empowerment packages

All registered members also get access to our basic training resources regardless of winning status.
