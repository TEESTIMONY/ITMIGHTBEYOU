Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting and communicating with our team.

What's the best way to contact your support team?

The fastest way to get assistance is through our email at support@itmightbeyou.ng. You can also call our support line at +234 800 000 0000 during business hours, or use the contact form on this page. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.

How can I verify if I've been selected as a winner?

All winners are notified via email and SMS immediately after the draw. You can also check the winners page on our website, which is updated after each draw. If you believe you've won but haven't been contacted, please reach out to our winner verification team at winners@itmightbeyou.ng or call our dedicated winner's hotline at +234 811 111 1111.

I'm having issues with my payment. Who should I contact?

For payment-related issues, please email payments@itmightbeyou.ng with details of the problem you're experiencing. Include your account ID, the date of the attempted payment, and any error messages you received. Alternatively, you can call our support line during business hours for immediate assistance.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We value your feedback and suggestions! You can share your thoughts through our contact form, by emailing feedback@itmightbeyou.ng, or by calling our office. We regularly review community input to improve our platform and services.
